# Quickstart Guide

Get up and running with GTX DEX in just a few steps. This guide will walk you through connecting your wallet, making your first trade, and earning your first yield.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- A compatible Web3 wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.)
- Some ETH or USDC for trading
- Basic understanding of decentralized trading

## Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit GTX DEX">
    Navigate to [gtxdex.xyz](https://gtxdex.xyz)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Wallet">
    Click "Connect Wallet" and select your preferred wallet provider
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign Transaction">
    Approve the connection request in your wallet
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Always verify you're on the official GTX DEX domain to avoid phishing attacks.
</Warning>

## Step 2: Fund Your Account

### Option 1: Deposit Directly
Transfer tokens directly to your wallet address. GTX DEX supports:
- ETH
- USDC
- USDT
- And many more ERC-20 tokens

### Option 2: Use the Faucet (Testnet)
For testing purposes, use our faucet to get test tokens:

```bash
# Visit the faucet page
https://gtxdex.xyz/faucet
```

## Step 3: Place Your First Order

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Market Order">
    1. Navigate to the Trading page
    2. Select your trading pair (e.g., ETH/USDC)
    3. Choose "Market" order type
    4. Enter the amount you want to trade
    5. Click "Buy" or "Sell"
    6. Confirm the transaction in your wallet
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Limit Order">
    1. Navigate to the Trading page
    2. Select your trading pair
    3. Choose "Limit" order type
    4. Set your desired price
    5. Enter the amount
    6. Click "Place Order"
    7. Confirm the transaction
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Step 4: Start Earning Yield

### Provide Liquidity
Earn fees by providing liquidity to trading pairs:

1. Go to the **Pools** section
2. Select a pool or create a new one
3. Add your tokens to the pool
4. Start earning trading fees and rewards

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Wallet won't connect">
    - Ensure you're using a supported wallet
    - Check that you're on the correct network
    - Try refreshing the page and reconnecting
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Transaction failed">
    - Check that you have sufficient gas fees
    - Verify token balances
    - Try increasing gas limit
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Orders not filling">
    - Check market liquidity
    - Verify order price is within market range
    - Consider using market orders for immediate execution
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
