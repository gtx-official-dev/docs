# 🚀 GTX (Great Trading Xperience)

## Earn Yield While You Trade

GTX is the first decentralized exchange that lets you **earn yield while you trade**. Built on a Central Limit Order Book (CLOB) architecture, GTX combines the efficiency of traditional trading with the yield generation opportunities of DeFi.

## Why GTX Changes Everything

Unlike traditional exchanges where your capital sits idle between trades, GTX ensures every moment your assets are working for you:

- **💰 Continuous Yield Generation**: Earn returns on your trading positions automatically
- **📊 Professional Trading**: CLOB-based order matching for precise price discovery  
- **🌐 Permissionless Markets**: Anyone can create new trading pairs
- **⚡ Capital Efficiency**: No impermanent loss, no idle capital

## Architecture Highlights

GTX's innovative CLOB system delivers superior performance:

### 🌳 **Red-Black Tree Price Levels**
- **O(log n) Operations** – Efficient price level management
- **Quick Best Bid/Ask Access** – Instant market data retrieval
- **Ordered Iteration** – Seamless market traversal

### 📜 **Optimized Order Queues**
- **FIFO Execution** – Fair order processing at each price level
- **Double-Linked Lists** – Efficient order management
- **Atomic Settlements** – Secure and consistent trade execution
